How You Can Help

Support BHARTIYA GORAKSHAN AND GAUSHALA

Your time, skills, supplies, sponsorship, and regular support can protect cows and strengthen our community work.

Volunteer Your Time and Skills

Support our daily activities by volunteering your time. Whether it is helping with administration, events, outreach programs, or on-site assistance, every contribution makes a difference.

Donate Funds

Your financial support helps us maintain our facilities, provide essential services, and continue our mission of serving the community and those in need.

Sponsor a Cow or Program

Become a sponsor and directly contribute to the care of a cow or the success of a specific program. Sponsorship helps cover food, healthcare, shelter, and other essential expenses.

Donate Supplies

Contribute useful items such as fodder, medicines, equipment, cleaning materials, office supplies, or other necessities that help us operate effectively.

Assist with Events and Campaigns

Help us organize and manage awareness programs, fundraising events, educational workshops, and community outreach initiatives.

Offer Professional Services

Professionals can support us by providing expertise in veterinary care, healthcare, education, legal assistance, accounting, technology, or marketing.

Help with Fundraising

Join our fundraising efforts by organizing donation drives, reaching out to supporters, or promoting campaigns within your network.

Spread Awareness

Share our mission and activities with your friends, family, and social media community. Increased awareness helps us reach more supporters and beneficiaries.

Partner with Us

Organizations, businesses, and institutions can collaborate with us through sponsorships, joint programs, donations, or other meaningful partnerships.

Become a Regular Supporter

Make a lasting impact by contributing regularly. Ongoing support helps us plan for the future and continue our work without interruption.

For Cow Shelter Support

Gaushala Support Options

Sponsor a Cow's Care

Help provide food, shelter, medical treatment, and daily care for a cow through monthly or annual sponsorship.

Support Veterinary Treatment

Contribute towards vaccinations, medical checkups, emergency treatments, and overall health care for rescued and sheltered cows.

Donate Fodder and Feed

Ensure that cows receive nutritious food by donating fodder, hay, feed, or funds specifically for feeding programs.

Support Rescue and Rehabilitation

Help us rescue abandoned, injured, or vulnerable cows and provide them with safe shelter and proper care.
